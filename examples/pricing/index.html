Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a Monthly Active User (MAU)?
A Monthly Active User is a unique user who authenticates at least once during a calendar month. If the same user authenticates 100 times in a month, they only count as 1 MAU.
Can I start with the free tier and upgrade later?
Absolutely! You can self-host for free forever, and upgrade to managed hosting when you're ready. Your data seamlessly migrates when you upgrade.
What happens if I exceed my MAU limit?
We'll notify you when you reach 80% of your limit. If you exceed it, we'll automatically upgrade you to the next tier (with 7 days notice). No surprise shutdowns or overage fees.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes! If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll refund you 100%. No questions asked.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime with one click. No long-term contracts or cancellation fees. Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We never store passwords (passwordless!). Private keys never leave user devices. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're working on SOC 2 compliance.
